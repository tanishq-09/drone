Airborne Platforms & Systems

At Tata Advanced Systems, we bring together world-class capabilities, cutting-edge infrastructure, and a highly skilled workforce to drive the local manufacturing and final assembly of both Fixed and

Rotary Wing platforms.


With a strong commitment to Make-in-India, we actively partner with global OEMs to bid for large-scale platform projects, aligning with the Ministry of Defence’s (MoD) vision. Our expertise ensures

seamless integration, advanced value addition, and superior quality, reinforcing India's self-reliance in aerospace and defence manufacturing.

UNMANNED AERIAL SYSTEMS

Tata Advanced Systems has developed several Unmanned Aerial Systems which

are already in use or undergoing trials with the Armed and Paramilitary Forces and

State Police Forces. We are focused on offering cutting edge solutions to meet

emerging Remotely Piloted Aircraft (RPA) / Unmanned Aerial Vehicle (UAV)

requirements from Defence and Security forces around the world.
